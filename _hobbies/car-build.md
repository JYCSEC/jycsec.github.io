Currently, I own a ‘15 370z Nismo 6M in the pearl white. It appealed to me because it was a Japanese NA V6 with a sporty heritage and awesome styling. Since I picked up the car back in March, I’ve done a few quality-of-life mods and some non-performance mods and have a lot more planned in the future. 
I'm debating whether I should stay with the platform given the expense of getting the horsepower numbers I want (650+ RWHP) and the appeal of some other platforms. As for Japanese cars though, I think the Z really stands on its own and if there was a different Japanese V6/V8 car with a manual transmission that hasn't been inflated to death with the bro-tax, the decision to jump platforms would be much easier. For now, only time will tell. 

I am looking into C6 Z06 'vettes, 6th gen GT350s, Gen 2,4,5 Vipers, Porshe 997.1 Turbos, Porshe 997.2 GT3s, and lastly, F360 Spiders.
